Game Rules

Objective

  • Capture territory by surrounding tiles.
  • The first player to capture the majority of tiles wins!

How to Play

  • Click or tap an empty corner of a tile to place a puck.
  • Click a vulnerable (not connected to any captured land) opponent's puck to remove it. Click again to take the spot.
  • Capture: Completely surround one or more tiles to claim them.

Mystery Boxes

  • Capture a pentagon with a Mystery Box to get a surprise.
  • Contents: Bonus moves (+1, +2 or +3) or a BOMB!
